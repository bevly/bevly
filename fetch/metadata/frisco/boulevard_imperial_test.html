Boulevard Imperial Stout

Brewery: Boulevard
Name: Imperial Stout
Brewery Location: Kansas City, Missouri
ABV: 11.8%
Serving Size: 10oz
Description: Imperial Stout - IBU: 63 - Like India Pale Ale, Imperial Stouts were originally brewed with high percentages of malt and hops to help them withstand the rigors of a long sea journey, not to India but to Imperial Russia and the Baltic States. This version is an over-the-top riff on the style, with a huge grain bill featuring several kinds of malted barley, wheat, rye, and oats. A portion of the batch has been aged in whiskey barrels, then blended with fresh ale before bottling/kegging.
